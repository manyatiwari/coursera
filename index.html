Self Love

Self love forms the foundation of your single, most important relationship - that with yourself. The strength of all your other relationships is exactly equal to the strength of that foundation. To love yourself is not just a self-esteem boosting piece of advice. It is the prerequisite to truly loving others.

Self Independent

Self-reliance is the ability of commitment and effort for providing the necessities of life for self and other family members. The habit of doing our own work ourselves without depending on others is called self-reliance. It is a noble quality in human nature and is found in the life of all great man and women.

Self Motivation

Self-Motivation is Empowering. Motivation is a very strong tool we can use to get further in life and to achieve our dreams and goals. In order for us to stay motivated we have to have a goal and be driven towards achieving that goal. Self-motivation drives people to keep going even in the face of set-backs.
